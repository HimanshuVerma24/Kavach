Privacy Policy for Kavach

1. Introduction

Kavach is a multipurpose Discord bot designed to help manage servers, provide fun commands, moderation tools, and other utilities to enhance user experience. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

2. Information We Collect

Kavach may collect the following information:

  • User IDs – to identify users interacting with the bot.
  • Server IDs – to manage server-specific settings and features.
  • Messages or Logs – only when necessary for moderation, commands, or bot functionality.
  • Database & Analytics Data – to store settings, preferences, and improve bot performance.

Kavach does not collect sensitive personal information such as real names, addresses, phone numbers, or payment details.

3. How We Use the Data

The data collected by Kavach is used for:

  • Providing core bot functionality and features.
  • Moderation tools to keep servers safe and organized.
  • Storing server and user preferences.
  • Improving the bot experience through analytics and performance monitoring.

4. Data Sharing

Kavach does not share your personal data with third parties. All collected data is used solely for the bot’s functionality and improvement.
